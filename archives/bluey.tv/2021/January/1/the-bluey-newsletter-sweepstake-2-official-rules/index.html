    Please read these Official Rules carefully before entering the Bluey Toy Sweepstakes (the “Sweepstakes”).

    NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED.

    1. Eligibility

    The Sweepstakes is open only to legal residents of the fifty (50) United States (including the District of Columbia) who are sixteen (16) years of age or older at the time of entry. Employees of BBC Studios Americas, Inc., its affiliates, promotional partners, as well as the immediate family (spouse, parents, siblings, and children) and household members of each of foregoing are not eligible to enter. The Sweepstakes is subject to federal, state, and local laws and regulations and is void where prohibited by law. If you are a deemed a minor in your state of residence, you must have the consent of your parent or legal guardian to participate in this Sweepstakes. Proof of consent may be required at any time.

    2. Sponsor

    The Sweepstakes is sponsored by BBC Studios Americas, Inc., 1120 Avenue of the Americas, FL 5, New York, NY 10036 (the “Sponsor”). The BBC Code of Conduct (the “Code”) for competitions can be found at https://www.bbc.com/editorialguidelines/guidance/code-of-conduct  and all Sponsor promotions comply with the Code.

    3. Agreement to Official Rules

    Participation in the Sweepstakes constitutes each entrant’s full and unconditional agreement to and acceptance of these Official Rules and the decisions of the Sponsor which are final and binding. Winning a prize is contingent upon fulfilling all requirements set forth herein in a timely manner.

    4. Entry Period

    The Sweepstakes shall consist of one (1) entry period as follows: beginning on June 17, 2020 and ends on June 20, 2020 at 11:59 PM EST. Entries received prior to or after an Entry Period will be disqualified. The use of any agencies or automated software to submit entries will void all entries submitted by that person.

    5. Entry

    During the Entry Period, answer the trivia question at bluey.tv (the “Trivia Question”) with the correct answer to the Trivia Question. There is a limit of one (1) entry per person. Subsequent entries will be void.

    6. Drawing

    Within forty-eight (48) hours of the end of each Entry Period, the Sponsor shall select a potential winner in a random drawing of all entries received. The odds of being selected depend on the number of eligible entries received. The Sponsor will attempt to notify the potential winner(s) within seventy-two (72) hours of the drawing. If a potential winner does not respond within seventy-two (72) hours after the first attempt to contact him/her, the Sponsor will select an alternate potential winner in his/her place at random from all entries received.

    7. Requirements of Potential Winners

    Except where prohibited, a potential winner may be required to complete and return an affidavit of eligibility and liability/publicity release (the “Affidavit/Release”) within fourteen (14) calendar days of being notified. If a potential winner fails to sign and return the Affidavit/Release within the required time period, an alternate entrant will be selected in his/her place in a random drawing of all entries received. If a prize is valued at $600 or more, the potential winner will be required to fill out and return a W-9 form within fourteen (14) calendar days of being notified.

    8. Prize

    The prize for the Sweepstakes in the Second Entry Period is one (1) of three (3) Bluey and Bingo mini plush sets each with an approximate retail value of Seven Dollars and Ninety Nine Cents ($7.99 USD). No cash or other substitution may be made, except by the Sponsor, who reserves the right to substitute a prize with another prize of equal or greater value if the advertised prize is not available for any reason as determined by the Sponsor in its sole discretion. The winner is responsible for all taxes associated with the prize. Once each winner is selected and notified, each winner shall not be entitled to transfer or sell the prize(s) to any other third party, even in the event that such third party would otherwise have been entitled to enter the Sweepstakes. Should a winner sell or attempt to sell its prize, the winner will be disqualified and Sponsor will select a new winner. Each prize is awarded “as is” and without warranty of any kind, express or implied, including, without limitation, any implied warranty of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose.

    9. Privacy

    All information submitted as part of this Sweepstakes will be treated in accordance with the Sponsor’s Privacy Policy (available at https://www.bbcstudios.com/privacy/). Sponsor will only ever use each entrant’s personal details for the purposes of administering the Sweepstakes and will not publish them or provide them to anyone without permission.

    Except where prohibited, participation in the Sweepstakes constitutes each winner’s consent to the Sponsor’s use of the winner’s name, likeness, voice, biographical information, and state of residence for promotional purposes in any media without further payment or consideration.

Sponsor reserves the right in its sole discretion to disqualify any individual it finds to be tampering with the entry process or the operation of the Sweepstakes or to be acting in violation of these Official Rules or in an unsportsmanlike or disruptive manner. Any attempt by any person to undermine the legitimate operation of the Sweepstakes may be a violation of criminal and civil law, and, should such an attempt be made, the Sponsor reserves the right to seek damages from any such person to the fullest extent permitted by law. The Sponsor’s failure to enforce any term of these Official Rules shall not constitute a waiver of that provision. If the identity of an entrant is disputed, the authorized email account holder at the time of entry will be deemed to be the entrant. An entrant may be required to provide proof that he/she is the authorized accountholder of the email account associated with the selected entry. The sole determinant of time for the purposes of receipt of a valid entry in the Sweepstakes will be the Sponsor’s computer. Sponsor reserves the right to amend these terms and conditions or to cancel, alter or amend the Official Rules or the Prize at any stage, if deemed necessary in its opinion, or if circumstances arise outside of its control.
