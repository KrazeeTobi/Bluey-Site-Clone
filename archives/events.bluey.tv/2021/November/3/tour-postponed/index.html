    • Tour Announcement

    Due to the ongoing impacts of COVID-19 including reductions in venue capacities and border restrictions, some Bluey’s Big Play shows in NSW, VIC and TAS were postponed.

    We’re excited to announce that the postponed shows will be back on sale across NSW, VIC & TAS.

    If you already had tickets for Bluey’s Big Play and your show was rescheduled, the venue or ticket provider will be in contact with you soon with more information about new dates. 

    If you already had tickets for Bluey’s Big Play and your tickets were refunded, the venue or ticket provider will be in contact with you to give you first access to the pre-sale when the shows go back on sale.

    If you haven’t already bought tickets and would like to buy tickets, sign up to receive Bluey’s Big Play tour updates and keep an eye on the Book now page to see which shows have gone back on sale.

    Thank you for being incredibly patient while we work to bring Bluey’s Big Play to your hometown. 

    All of our Bluey extended family wish you well and we can’t wait to welcome you back to theatres across the country soon.

    *Last updated on 4 November, 2021
