    Announcement Regarding COVID-19

    Due to announcements by the Australian Government related to COVID-19, Bluey’s Big Play’s Australian Tour will be delayed. It is currently being rescheduled with the plan being to commence later in the year.

    Even though many performances have sold out, don’t worry – tickets already purchased are guaranteed and will be transferable to the new dates. We ask you to hold on to your tickets while we make the new arrangements.

    Many venues are currently closed with staff working remotely, but please be assured that all ticket holders will be contacted directly as soon as possible with further information.

    We thank you for your ongoing patience as we work hard to reschedule all 53 venues across Australia.

    All of our Bluey extended family wish you well. We can’t wait to see you soon!!
