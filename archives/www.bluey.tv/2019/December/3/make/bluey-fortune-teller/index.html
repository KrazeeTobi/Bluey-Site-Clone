    Will the future be boring or will you have to be brave? Find out by making our awesome Bluey fortune teller.

    Download the template and follow the step-by-step instructions below!

    1. Fold up all four corners so that the points meet in the middle.
    2. Turn the paper over and fold in all four corners so that the points meet in the middle.
    3. Fold it in half.
    4. Push your fingers into the corners to work it into shape.
    5. Play and discover your fortune!
