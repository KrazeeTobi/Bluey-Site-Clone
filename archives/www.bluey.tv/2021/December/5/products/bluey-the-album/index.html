    Bluey The Album

    Bluey The Album features 17 songs, all original compositions from Season 1 including music from fan favourite episodes such as ‘Keepy Uppy’, ‘Here Come The Grannies!’, ‘Pool’ and ‘The Creek’.

    Two new version of the catchy theme tune are included as an extended version and ‘Instrument Parade’.

    The first half of the album features upbeat songs to get the whole family dancing like ‘The Claw’ and ‘Taxi’. The second half takes a tranquil tone with highlights ‘I Know A Place (The Creek Song)’, vocals by Helena Czajka & Jazz D’arcy, and ‘Camping’.

    This is the perfect album for Bluey fans of any age.

    The CD comes with a sticker sheet featuring 4 colourful stickers. Presented in a gatefold digi sleeve with original cover artwork specially designed for this release by Ludo Studio, the makers of Bluey.
