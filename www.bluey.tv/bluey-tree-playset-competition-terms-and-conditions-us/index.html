There is a limit of one (1) Entry per person. Additional entries will be void. 

All Entries must be received by 11:59 PM ET of the last date of the Competition, i.e. January 29, 2023. Entries which are incomplete, ineligible or which contain an incorrect answer to the trivia question posed shall be disqualified (“Disqualified Entries”). You may only enter once.
